Procedure

  • 1

    Select a Vehicle from the website

    It's quite simple. At first, select a vehicle you want. Next, get in touch by filling in the contact form or email us at {% if default_email %} {{ default_email }} {% else %} info@globaltrademotors.com {% endif %} to get a proforma invoice. The proforma invoice will be sent you by e-mail after confirming your inquiry.

  • 2

    Print the Performa Invoice and goto your bank

    Print the proforma invoice and go to your bank to make payment by telegraphic transfer (Swift Transfer). And send us a copy of bank receipt by either E-mail , or take the copy to our local agent in Maputo.

  • 3

    We book shipment to Maputo

    We book space for your car on a vessel as soon as we confirm your payment. When the space is booked, we will send you Booking confirmation e-mail stating the dates of Shipment and Arrival.
